# bert_config/

Configs and tokenizer files for the language encoders used by the VLN-BERT
backbone. The names must match — they are referenced by
`vlnce_baselines/models/etp/vlnbert_init.py`.

Expected layout:

    bert_config/
    ├── bert-base-uncased/             # used for R2R-CE (English-only)
    │   ├── config.json
    │   └── vocab.txt
    └── xlm-roberta-base/              # used for RxR-CE (multilingual)
        ├── config.json
        ├── tokenizer.json             # (or sentencepiece.bpe.model + tokenizer_config.json)
        └── ... (any extra HuggingFace artefacts)

Download from HuggingFace:
  - https://huggingface.co/bert-base-uncased
  - https://huggingface.co/xlm-roberta-base
