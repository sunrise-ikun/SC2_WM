# data/scene_datasets/

Place the **Matterport3D (MP3D)** scene meshes here. Access requires signing
the Matterport3D Terms of Use:

    https://niessner.github.io/Matterport/

Expected layout:

    data/scene_datasets/
    └── mp3d/
        ├── 17DRP5sb8fy/
        ├── 1LXtFkjw3qL/
        ├── ...
        └── zsNo4HB9uLZ/

Each scene directory contains the `.glb`/`.navmesh` files used by Habitat-Sim.
The path is referenced by `SCENES_DIR` in:
  - run_r2r/r2r_vlnce.yaml
  - run_rxr/rxr_vlnce.yaml
