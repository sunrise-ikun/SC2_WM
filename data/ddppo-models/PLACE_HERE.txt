# data/ddppo-models/

Place the **DDPPO-pretrained depth encoder** here. It is referenced by
`MODEL.DEPTH_ENCODER.ddppo_checkpoint` in
`vlnce_baselines/config/default.py`.

Expected file:

    data/ddppo-models/
    └── gibson-2plus-resnet50.pth

Source: https://github.com/facebookresearch/habitat-lab (DDPPO point-nav
pretrained models) or the project Google Drive release.
