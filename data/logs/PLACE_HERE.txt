# data/logs/

Runtime output directory. Created/populated automatically during training and
evaluation. Nothing needs to be downloaded into this folder.

Sub-directories created at runtime:

    data/logs/
    ├── checkpoints/<exp_name>/        # iterative training checkpoints
    ├── tensorboard_dirs/<exp_name>/   # tensorboard event files
    ├── eval_results/<exp_name>/       # evaluation metrics dumps
    ├── video/<exp_name>/              # rendered episode videos (if enabled)
    └── running_log/<exp_name>_*.log   # text logs from `habitat.logger`

Paths are configured by `TENSORBOARD_DIR`, `CHECKPOINT_FOLDER`, `RESULTS_DIR`,
and `VIDEO_DIR` in:
  - run_r2r/iter_train.yaml
  - run_rxr/iter_train.yaml
